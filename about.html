Our Story

Who We Are

Founded over a decade ago, Kanana has grown from a small local contractor to one of the region's most trusted construction firms. Our journey has been built on a foundation of quality craftsmanship, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to our clients.

We specialize in both residential and commercial construction, bringing together expert architects, engineers, and craftspeople to deliver projects that exceed expectations. Every structure we build reflects our dedication to precision, innovation, and sustainability.

Today, with over 1500+ units delivered and a 98% on-time delivery rate, we continue to set new standards in the construction industry.
